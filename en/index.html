2019

What is it?

It is a daily event, where the undersigned publishes a post containing the question of the day, from 6am to 12pm and if it is not published in time, it will be postponed.

The idea was born on July 30, 2019, taking inspiration from one who asked the questions of the day in the video about frequency, catching the ball and serving, first of all, to revive Prismando (my main group), that of the participants' activities continued to fall:

Buongiorno mondo! Come va? Come state? Svegliati bene? Io bene, grazie!

The next day, I reworked it again, making it more bizarre:

Buongiorno a tuuuutttiiiiiiii!!! Come va? Come state? Famiglia tutto a posto? Pure il lavoro e persino i vostri occhietti appena svegli?

On August 11th, Gooday of Stegon was officially born. Initially, it was an exclusive of the Prismando groups, but after its success on Telegram, it was expanded into some external groups and, on August 17th, also on Ludomedia through a dedicated room, during a discussion with one if the event could be expanded to outside of Telegram.

Before 2 October 2019, every question of the day published on Ludomedia is the one of the previous day on Telegram, by priority.

On October 8th, Gooday of Stegon expands even more towards English, to satisfy those who do not know Italian.
