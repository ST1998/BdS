- A another PC with Linux Mint -


Post of Apr. 29, 2020

As you can see, this is a generic PC made of HP-branded aluminum with the soul of Linux Mint. It is a gift for my mother, since she wanted a PC of her own, in addition to wanting to use Zoom, so that she didn't have to use her Smartphone every time for my little sister's school lessons.

The (my) decision to switch from Windows 10 to Linux Mint stems from the fact that Zoom for Windows 10 caused the total freeze of the operating system, in an important moment such as following the lesson, which made my mother very angry and is there that I came into play.

After installing Linux Mint, I configured it with my golden hands, so that it can have everything it needed, also adding something of mine. Not only did Zoom not give more problems, but both the operating system and the programs themselves work better than Windows 10, so much so that my mother got on well, even my little sister, which made me quite happy.

It is also used to listen to music and, given that the PC has bad speakers, which return too low and croaking sound, we have solved the problem with a 10 euro Bluetooth Speaker, where Linux Mint recognized it at first sight and it manages to return a cleaner sound with decent bass, ha ha!
